# Cristal Baschet Drawing Brief

No dimensioned drawings exist yet. This brief names the first drawings a
builder would need once the one-rod bench rig produces measured evidence
(see design.md L2 Boundary and First Measurement Gates).

Required first drawings (blocked on measurement):

- One-rod bench rig: rod support, stem coupling, and mass-stack interface at
  coupon scale (design.md CL-001 through CL-004 categories).
- Frame rail mockup showing rod, stem, and swappable-radiator mounting points
  without committing to a full sculptural frame layout (CL-009).
- Radiator coupon comparison sheet: cone, flap, and neutral reference modules
  on a shared stem termination (CL-005 through CL-007).
- Whisker safety coupon: containment and edge-handling detail only
  (CL-008); no whisker count, length, or spacing until safety review clears.

## Authority Notes

- No design table (xlsx) or measured reference exists for this instrument.
  Every dimension referenced above is `pending_measurement` until a coupon
  test or reviewed reference sets it.
- Do not produce a full-frame or fabrication drawing before the one-rod rig,
  stem-coupling test, and radiator swap comparisons in `validation.csv` are
  complete.
- `cad/cristal-baschet.scad` (if present) is a non-dimensional frame/rod-array
  envelope only — it does not model rod tuning, stem thread geometry, or
  radiator acoustics, and is not drawing authority.
